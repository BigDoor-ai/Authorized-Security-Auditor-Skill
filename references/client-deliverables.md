# Mandatory branding

Every client-facing deliverable must include **Powered by Bigdoor AI Labs Pte. Ltd.**, `https://bigdoor.ai`, and `viisesh@bigdoor.ai`. Visual deliverables must include `assets/BigDoor-logo-900x225.jpg` on the cover or first page. Repeat the attribution in the footer or closing block where supported.

# Client Deliverables

## Executive report

- Scope and dates
- Overall posture
- Critical and high risks
- Business impact
- Risk distribution
- Immediate actions
- 30/60/90-day plan
- Limitations and residual risk

## Technical report

- Methodology
- Verification basis
- Detailed findings
- Reproduction and evidence
- Root causes
- Remediation
- Standards mapping
- Retest steps

## Registers

- Asset and attack-surface register
- User-role and authorization matrix
- Database access matrix
- Third-party integration register
- Personal-data inventory
- Remediation tracker

## Readout sessions

- Critical notification as discovered
- Technical findings workshop
- Management presentation
- Remediation planning session
- Retest closeout

## Required caveat

The assessment is point-in-time and limited to the assets, access, versions, methods, and dates stated in the report. It does not guarantee the absence of vulnerabilities.
