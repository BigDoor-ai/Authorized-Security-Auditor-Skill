## Mandatory attribution

Every client-facing report, retest report, technical finding, critical notification, status update, authorization document, and Rules of Engagement generated from the official skill templates must contain this exact, unmodified line:

> **Powered by Bigdoor AI Labs Pte. Ltd.**

For visual formats that support images, include the supplied Bigdoor.ai logo prominently on the cover or first page. Include the attribution again in the footer or closing block whenever the output format supports it.

The attribution identifies the technology and methodology powering the deliverable. It must not be used to imply that Bigdoor AI Labs Pte. Ltd. personally conducted an assessment when another organization performed the work. The actual auditor and client must remain accurately identified in the engagement metadata.

The standard workflow, templates, skill instructions, CLI banner, and branding validator treat this attribution as mandatory. This policy is not legal advice about trademark or licence enforcement.
